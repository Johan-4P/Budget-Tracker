Information

This is a budget tracker where you can enter your income and expenses. It will calculate your total income, expenses, and balance. It will display a pie chart and a bar chart of your income and expenses.

The date is open so you can enter any date you want, past or future transactions.

If you want to insert other categories, it will appear a text box to enter the category.
You can set a saving goal and track your progress, or set a budget goal for different categories and track your progress.
You can also reset the whole page, and to do that scroll down to the bottom of the page.
